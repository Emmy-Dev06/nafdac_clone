NAFDAC Consumer Safety Club

The NAFDAC Consumer Safety Club (NCSC) is a public enlightenment and sensitization platform of the Agency used in the fight against fake/substandard regulated products, and to promote the ‘quality culture’ in Nigerian youth. The objectives of NCSC are to: Reorient the youth and redirect their energy away from social vices and anti-social behavior towards healthy habits and meaningful development.  Serve as a platform for the promotion of NAFDAC’s fight against fake drugs and other substandard products. Use members as foot soldiers for the reinvigoration of NAFDAC’s anti-drug abuse and misuse campaign. Assist with information flow on NAFDAC’s regulatory activities.
